Site Plan for [Bread by Tens]

Site Name

Bread by Tens

This is the name I chose because my name is Tenna and my nickname is Tens. I am the bread baker so it made sense that I name my bread business "Bread By Tens"

Site Purpose

This site is to be used as a tool for me to help inform people of the bread that I sell. I want to include a form that people can send suggestions about different flavors of sourdough I should try. I also want a form that people can submit to join a newsletter where I will send updates about when I am selling my bread. I will include pictures and an about me section so they are know what kind of bread I make and the quality of it.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: What kind of bread is being made and what flavors of bread?
  • Scenario 2: How will I know when the bread is for sale?
