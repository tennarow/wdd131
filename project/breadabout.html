About Bread By Tens

Hello! My name is Tenna King. I was born and raised in Mesa, Arizona. I live here now with my husband and daughter. I am currently a nanny for a 5 month old baby and I bring 18 month daughter to work. My husband and I are expecting another girl in October. I am a foodie to the core. I have always loved experiementing in the kitchen. Sourdough baking has allowed me to do just that! My love for food and creating have made something magical and I love to share my bread with the world.

My sourdough began on the other side of the world! I lived in Slovenia for 18 months and while I was there I met a friend who shared her starter with me. My first few loafs were not very pretty, but that is where it all began. I still use the same Slovenian starter today!
